Kobo Reader

Working with the Kobo Libra H2O
Updated: December 4, 2019

Convert EPUBs to KEPUBs with kepubify. A KEPUB (.kepub.epub) allows the Kobo reader to display estimated time remaining among other nice to haves. More info can be found at the mobileread wiki.

Moving files onto the Kobo.

sudo pmount /dev/sdx
cp <files> /media/sdx/library
umount /dev/sdx
