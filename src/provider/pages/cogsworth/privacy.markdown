Cogsworth is a private family calendar and reminder service operated by Kyle
Ondy for personal, household use. This policy explains what information the
Cogsworth SMS reminder line collects and how it is used.

## What this service does

A family member sends a text message to the Cogsworth number. The message is
turned into a reminder shown on a shared family display, and the service texts
back a short confirmation. Participation is limited to a small set of known
family phone numbers.

## Information collected

When you text the Cogsworth number, the service receives and stores:

- Your mobile phone number.
- The content of the messages you send.
- The date and time each message was received.

No location data, contacts, or other information is collected.

## How the information is used

Your phone number and message content are used only to create and display the
reminders you send, and to send you a confirmation reply. The information is
stored on a private server used solely to run this household service.

## Sharing

Your phone number and messages are never sold, rented, or shared with any
third party for marketing purposes. No mobile information is shared with third
parties or affiliates for their own promotional use. Information may be
disclosed only where required by law.

## Message frequency and opt-out

Message frequency depends on how often you text the service. The service
replies only to messages you send. Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, at which point the service will
stop sending you messages. Reply HELP for assistance.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to Kyle Ondy at kyle@ondy.org.
