These terms cover the Cogsworth SMS reminder line, a private service operated
by Kyle Ondy for personal, household use.

## The service

Cogsworth is a family calendar and reminder service. When an approved family
member texts the Cogsworth number, the message is turned into a reminder shown
on a shared family display and the service replies with a short confirmation.
Access is limited to a small set of known family phone numbers; this is not a
public or commercial service.

## Consent

By texting the Cogsworth number, you agree to receive SMS reminder
confirmations at the number you send from. You may stop at any time.

## Message frequency and charges

Message frequency depends on how often you text the service. The service
replies only to messages you send. Message and data rates may apply according
to your mobile plan and carrier.

## Opt-out and help

Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages from the service. Reply HELP
for assistance. Supported carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered
messages.

## Privacy

Your phone number and messages are handled as described in the

shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
